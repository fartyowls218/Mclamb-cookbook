Down-home Cooking with The McLamb Family

My sister, Serena Jones, put together a family cookbook once upon a time. The recipes were based on hand-me-down recipes of the family as well as tried and true published recipes. The hand-me-downs were primarily from:

  • our mother, Jean McLamb Jones
  • our grandmother, Lucy Pearl McLamb (Ma)
  • our Aunt Pearl, Pearl McLamb Page

This is a faithful reproduction of that cookbook.
